About the Project

GastroScanAI is an innovative explainable AI (XAI) system designed to assist medical professionals in detecting early signs of stomach cancer from endoscopy images. Our solution combines deep learning with visual explanation techniques to provide not just predictions, but understandable insights into the AI's decision-making process.

Unlike traditional black-box AI systems, GastroScanAI highlights suspicious areas in endoscopy images using Grad-CAM technology, allowing doctors to understand why the system flagged certain regions while maintaining ultimate diagnostic authority.

Why It Matters

Stomach cancer is the fifth most common cancer worldwide, with over 1 million new cases annually. Tragically, many cases are detected at late stages when treatment options are limited and survival rates are low.

GastroScanAI addresses two critical challenges: the shortage of specialist gastroenterologists in rural and low-resource areas, and the need for more transparent AI systems that doctors can trust and understand.

Our solution is designed to be lightweight and accessible, requiring minimal computational resources, making it ideal for clinics with limited infrastructure.

⚠️

Early detection of stomach cancer can improve 5-year survival rates from 30% to over 90%
